# Contributing Guidelines

pysorteddict started out as a personal challenge to figure out how to build a feature-complete extension module for
Python. It remains a personal hobby project driven by my interests. For now, I am not actively accepting code
contributions.

Clear and reproducible bug reports and issues are welcome. Feel free to open an issue to discuss your ideas or
suggestions.

Pull requests (especially those which make major changes, refactor logic, or alter the architecture of the project) may
be closed without a review in order to keep the project manageable in its present state.

I may open pysorteddict to broader contributions in the future, after it has reached a state I am satisfied with. Until
then, as a recreational programmer, I prefer to keep development enjoyable and focused.

So, just be cool (optional).
